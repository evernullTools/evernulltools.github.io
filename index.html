SlashForge — Combat Trail Creator

SlashForge is a Unity tool for creating customizable combat trails for weapons, melee attacks, and stylized action effects.

It is designed for fast setup, editable presets, and deep visual control, making it easier to create sword trails, elemental slashes, fiery swipe kicks and other combat motion effects directly inside Unity.
